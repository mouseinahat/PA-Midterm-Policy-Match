Methodology

This MVP converts user answers and candidate issue positions into the same 12-axis policy vector.

How scoring works

Each answer is converted to a value from -2 to +2. Candidate positions are also modeled from -2 to +2. The app calculates distance on each shared axis and converts the average distance into a similarity percentage.

Production requirement: every candidate score should cite a campaign page, public statement, voting record, or reputable source.
