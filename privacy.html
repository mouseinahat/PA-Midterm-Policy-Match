Privacy-first MVP

Privacy approach

This prototype runs entirely in the browser and does not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or political profiles on a server.

Current v0

  • No account system
  • No server database
  • No personal identifier collection
  • No result upload
  • No real anti-bot protection yet

Production direction

  • Anonymous participation key
  • Issue-level one-response counting
  • Cloudflare Turnstile or similar bot protection
  • Rate limiting
  • Separate trusted vs. suspicious aggregate statistics
